# What does 'anti-steelman' mean in this context

## Context and Problem Statement

Question category: Anti-steelman.

## Considered Options

- **Weakest version of each side's own argument** — For each speaker, identify the most embarrassing / least defensible version of what they actually said. Used to flag where they're vulnerable to attack. Pairs naturally with steelman (best version) for self-criticism.
- **Strongest version of the opposing argument** — For each speaker, write the most defensible form of the OTHER speaker's position. This is just steelman-from-the-other-side. Some people use 'anti-steelman' as a synonym for 'steelman the opposition.'
- **Strawman / worst-case attack against each side** — Identify the worst attack each side could face (regardless of whether it's fair). Surfaces failure modes a hostile reader might exploit. More forensic than collaborative.

## Decision Outcome

Chose **Weakest version of each side's own argument**. For each speaker, identify the most embarrassing / least defensible version of what they actually said. Used to flag where they're vulnerable to attack. Pairs naturally with steelman (best version) for self-criticism.
